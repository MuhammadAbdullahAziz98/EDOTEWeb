Keep in touch with your elders!

Healthcare! Because all lives matter.

EDOTE is an intelligent healthcare monitoring system for monitoring of health of elderly patients with cardiovascular diseases. It used machine learning, deep learning and cloud computing in order to proide emergency services after detection of heart attack or in case of fall.
